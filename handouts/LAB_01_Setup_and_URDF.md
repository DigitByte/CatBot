Prepared by D. Hoskia


## Goal
Bring up the CatBot simulation and interpret the URDF model.

## Steps
1. Follow `setup.md`.
2. Launch the test simulation:
   `roslaunch gait_control gait_control_test.launch`
3. Open RViz and inspect:
   - robot model
   - frame tree

## Deliverables
- Screenshot of RViz with CatBot loaded
- Short writeup: What does the URDF define? (3–5 sentences)
- Command log (copy/paste)

## Rubric (10 pts)
- 3: Build and launch success
- 3: RViz screenshot with model and frames
- 2: URDF explanation
- 2: Clean submission
